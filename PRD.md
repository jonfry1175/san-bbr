# Product Requirements Document: Website Redevelopment for PT. Karya Halim Sampoerna

## 1. Objective

To redevelop the corporate website for PT. Karya Halim Sampoerna (`karyahalimsampoerna.id`). The new website must include all features from the existing site while adopting a new, modern, and professional design identity inspired by `https://www.pttun.co.id/`. The goal is to create a user-friendly, responsive, and professional online presence that effectively showcases the company's services, projects, and corporate identity.

## 2. Design and Branding Guidelines (Inspired by pttun.co.id)

- **Overall Feel:** The design should be professional, trustworthy, clean, and modern. It must convey a strong corporate identity.
- **Color Palette:**
  - **Primary Color:** Corporate Blue (e.g., `#0D2A4C` or similar) for headers, footers, and key branding elements.
  - **Secondary/Accent Color:** Gold/Yellow (e.g., `#FFC107` or similar) for call-to-action buttons, highlights, and icons.
  - **Neutral Colors:** White (`#FFFFFF`) and Light Gray (`#F8F9FA`) for page backgrounds and content sections to ensure a clean and readable layout.
- **Typography:**
  - **Headings:** Use a clean, modern sans-serif font (e.g., Poppins, Montserrat, or Lato) for all page titles and section headers.
  - **Body Text:** Use a highly readable sans-serif font (e.g., Open Sans, Roboto, or Lato) for paragraphs and general content.
- **Layout:**
  - Implement a structured, grid-based layout to ensure content is well-organized.
  - Utilize ample white space to prevent visual clutter and improve readability.
  - The design must be fully responsive, providing an optimal viewing experience across desktops, tablets, and mobile devices.

## 3. Functional Requirements (Features)

The new website must contain the following pages and features:

### 3.1. Global Elements

- **Header:**
  - Company Logo (Top-left).
  - Navigation Menu (links listed in 3.2).
  - Search Bar functionality.
  - Language Switcher (Bahasa Indonesia / English).
  - Prominent "Let's Talk" or "Contact Us" Call-to-Action button.
- **Footer:**
  - Company Logo and brief description.
  - Contact Information: Full Address, Phone Number, Email.
  - Quick Links to key pages.
  - Social Media Links (Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, Dribbble/Instagram).
  - Copyright information.

### 3.2. Pages and Specific Features

- **1. Home Page:**
  - Hero section with a compelling headline and a "Get Started Now" button.
  - Introduction to the company.
  - **Our Services Section:** A summary of key services offered (e.g., Aspal Hotmix, Eartwork, CTRB, etc.) with icons and brief descriptions.
  - **Our Works (Portfolio) Showcase:** A preview gallery of featured projects.
  - **Our Team Section:** A brief introduction to the team.
  - **News/Blog Section:** Display the 3 most recent articles.
  - Call-to-Action section encouraging users to get in touch.

- **2. About Us Page:**
  - Detailed company history, vision, and mission.
  - Introduction to the key team members with photos and brief bios.
  - Corporate values or company philosophy.

- **3. Our Works Page (Portfolio):**
  - A comprehensive gallery of all completed projects.
  - Projects should be filterable by category (e.g., road construction, earthwork).
  - Each project should have its own detail page with a description, image gallery, and key project details.

- **4. Gallery Page:**
  - A dedicated page for high-resolution images and videos showcasing company activities, equipment, and project sites.

- **5. News Page (Blog):**
  - A chronological listing of all company news, articles, and updates.
  - Each article should have its own dedicated page.
  - Include a sidebar with recent posts or categories.

- **6. Career Page:**
  - List of current job openings.
  - Each job opening should include a description, requirements, and an application method (e.g., email address or application form).

- **7. Contact Us Page:**
  - Contact Form with fields for Name, Email, Subject, and Message.
  - Full contact details: Address, Phone Number(s), Email Address.
  - Embedded Google Maps showing the office location.

## 4. Non-Functional Requirements

- **Performance:** The website must load quickly (e.g., under 3 seconds). Images should be optimized for the web.
- **SEO (Search Engine Optimization):** The website should be built with SEO best practices, including proper use of title tags, meta descriptions, header tags, and clean URLs.
- **Security:** Implement basic security measures to protect the website from common vulnerabilities.
- **Content Management System (CMS):** The website should be built on a user-friendly CMS (e.g., WordPress) to allow the internal team to easily update content, news, and job openings.
